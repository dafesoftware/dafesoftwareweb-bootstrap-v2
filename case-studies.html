Nurse Ready

Nurse Ready is a new way for internationally educated nurses (IENs) to learn about communicative strategies for the Canadian healthcare setting. Through authentic scenarios, this task-based self-assessment targets professional language and communication skills to offer insights into how communication impacts the client-centred experience in Canada. Designed in response to COVID-19, the app focuses on communication skills for high-stakes situations, such as managing challenging interactions during an infectious disease outbreak.
