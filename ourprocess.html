Our practice

Agile set of principles and practices that emphasizes
iterative development and collaboration

Our software development process at Dafe Software aligns well with the principles of Agile methodology. We build with the Agile set of principles and practices that emphasizes iterative development, collaboration, and continuous improvement that helps to ensure that the software is developed quickly, efficiently, and meets our client's needs and is also reflected in our flexible payment model.

Our Process

Our software development process is iterative, while constantly getting and implementing feedback from users and stakeholders incorporated into subsequent development cycles, which makes our software development process dynamic and customer-focused.

Flexibility in payment model

Our flexible payment plan aligns with Agile's customer-centric approach. By offering installment payments at different phases of development, we accommodate the diverse needs of your clients and stakeholders, which is essential for fostering a positive client relationship.

Implementation

Our implementation process involves the actual coding and programming of the software in this phase. Our Developers write the code according to the chosen programming language(s) and frameworks, following coding best practices and adhering to any established coding standards.

Testing

Various testing techniques, such as unit testing, integration testing, and system testing, are employed to ensure that the software functions correctly and meets the specified requirements. Bugs and issues are identified and resolved during this phase.

Design

The software's architecture and overall design are established during this phase. This includes determining the system's structure, data models, user interfaces, and any necessary algorithms or components.

Deployment

The software is prepared for release and deployed to the intended environment, such as production servers or end-user devices. This may involve activities like configuration management, data migration, and setting up necessary infrastructure.

Maintenance and Support

Once the software is live, we work on providing ongoing maintenance and support. This includes addressing bug fixes, implementing updates or enhancements, and providing user assistance or technical support.

Requirement Gathering

Our team's primary focus is understanding the needs and objectives of the software project by gathering requirements from stakeholders, users, and clients.

Planning

Our development team creates a plan outlining the project scope, timelines, resources required, and any potential risks or challenges.
